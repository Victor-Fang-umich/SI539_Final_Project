New Update in 2026!

A new update for Animal Crossing: New Horizons will be released on January 15, 2026. The free Version 3.0 update will add exciting new content for all players.

In addition, a special edition for the Nintendo Switch 2 will launch, featuring upgraded graphics and improved controls.

New Villagers

The upcoming Version 3.0 update for Animal Crossing: New Horizons will introduce four new villagers to the game.

Mineru and Tulin are inspired by The Legend of Zelda, while Cece and Viché are collaborations with Splatoon.

New Items

The upcoming Version 3.0 update will also bring a variety of new items and clothing, which gives players more ways to decorate their islands and dress their characters.
