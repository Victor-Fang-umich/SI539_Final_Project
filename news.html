New Update in 2026!

The newest update for Animal Crossing: New Horizons was released on January 15, 2026. The free Version 3.0 update added exciting new content for all players.

In addition, a special edition for the Nintendo Switch 2 launched, featuring upgraded graphics and improved controls.

Click to watch the official Animal Crossing trailer on YouTube

New Villagers

The Version 3.0 update for Animal Crossing: New Horizons introduced four new villagers to the game.

Cece and Viché were collaborations with Splatoon, while Mineru and Tulin were inspired by The Legend of Zelda.

New Items and Outfits

The Version 3.0 update also brought a variety of new items and outfits, giving players more ways to decorate their islands and dress their characters.
